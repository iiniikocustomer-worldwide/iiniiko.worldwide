WHAT IS IINIKO?

Stemming directly from rich Yoruba culture, IINIKO captures the precise and profound ontological meaning of "being born during troubled times."

Embraced and carried forward through layers of history and continuing to thrive in modern high-fashion and street expression, the name serves as an unbreakable armor—a direct testament to the strength found in struggle.
