INIKO MEANS:

BEING BORN DURING TROUBLED TIMES

Stemming from the Yoruba culture, IINIKO encapsulates the meaning of being born during troubled times. Embraced throughout history and continuing to thrive in modern-day usage, the name IINIKO serves as a testament to the strength and perseverance of those who bear it.
